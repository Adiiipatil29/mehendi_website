Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does mehendi last?
A: Mehendi typically lasts 1-2 weeks, depending on skin type and aftercare.

Q: How can I make my mehendi darker?
A: Keep the mehendi on for as long as possible and avoid washing with soap for the first 24 hours.
